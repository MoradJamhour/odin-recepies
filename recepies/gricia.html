Let's Make Some Gricia!

Ok, bur what is a Gricia?

A Pasta Gricia is basically a Carbonara without eggs.

Ingredients

  • 400g of rigatoni
  • 200g of roman pecorino
  • 280g of guanciale
  • pepper as needed

Steps

  1. Cook the guanciale till it's crispy
  2. Boil the water, salt it and cook the spaghetti (keep some cooking water)
  3. Once cooked add the spaghetti, the water you kept aside and plenty of pecorino
  4. Add as much pepper as you want
  5. Enjoy the second best dish you'll ever eat.
