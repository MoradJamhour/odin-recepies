Let's Make Some Pasta Burro and Salvia!

Ok, bur what is Pasta burro and salvia?

It's just a pasta dish you cook when you want something tasty but easy.

Ingredients

  • 400g of penne
  • 200g of pecorino
  • 200g of butter
  • 15 salvia leaves

Steps

  1. Cook the the pasta in boiling salted water
  2. Melt the butter in a pan
  3. Cook the leaves in the melted butter
  4. Add the cooked pasta and all the parmigiano
  5. Enjoy!
