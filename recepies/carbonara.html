Let's Make Some Carbonara!

Ok, bur what is a Carbonara?

A Carbonara is a beautifully tasty Roman pasta dish, with deeply rooted origins, great affordability, and a not so complicated recepie.

Ingredients

  • 400g of spaghetti
  • 200g of roman pecorino
  • 280g of guanciale
  • 5 egg yolks
  • pepper as needed

Steps

  1. Cook the guanciale till it's crispy
  2. Boil the water, salt it and cook the spaghetti (keep some cooking water)
  3. Whisk the eggs with some pepper some pecorino and some cooking water
  4. Once cooked add the spaghetti, the water you kept aside and plenty of pecorino
  5. Add as much pepper as you want
  6. Enjoy the best dish you'll ever eat.
